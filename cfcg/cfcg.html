Columbia University's Center for Complicated Grief researches solutions and therapy methods for resolving a person's grief. The Center approached Adobe with the prospect of redesigning the website's user experience to help better communicate their brand message. My role on the team spanned designing the user experience of three key areas: the therapist location, contact confirmation, and menu transitions. Each design presented their own challenges in finding interaction patterns that make sense. I also helped research opportunities in the website to convert users to paying members.

Project Name : The Center for Complicated Grief

Type : User Experience, User Interface, Interaction Design

Tools Used : Adobe XD, Sketch, Principle
