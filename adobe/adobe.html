The Kodak Moments Observatory was an exhibition held at the 2016 SXSW festival. Collaborating with director Marcos Lutyens, the installation features a complete sensory experience based on a photo the attendee provides. I cut together a trailer in Premiere Pro with After Effects elements to capture the narrative of the event. I also created interactive on-boarding for the app supporting the event.

Project Name : Kodak Moments Observatory

Type : Narrative Film making, User Interface, Exhibition Design

Tools Used : Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator,
